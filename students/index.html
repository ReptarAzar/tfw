About

Each Fall more than 50 graduate and undergraduate students from the the S.I. Newhouse School of Public Communications at Syracuse University canvas a small town in upstate New York to record, film, photograph and tell the stories of that community. This year the town of choice is Liverpool.

BY BOB MILLER

Liverpool High School student Richard Gaffney is a paradox. As the lone male cheerleader on the Liverpool High School Maroons cheer leading squad, he represents the emotions and struggles that define our high school years. Liverpool High School student Richard Gaffney is a paradox.
